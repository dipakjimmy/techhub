Quantity Estimation

ASISA delivers highly accurate Quantity Estimation services powered by BIM, enabling precise material take-offs (MTOs), reliable cost forecasting, and streamlined procurement planning. Using advanced BIM workflows, we extract quantities in real time, automate BOQ creation, and significantly reduce material waste—resulting in better cost control and smarter resource a llocation. By combining intelligent 3D models with robust estimation processes, we help clients achieve superior accuracy, lower project risks, and enhanced overall construction efficiency.
